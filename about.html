About ARK

Disruptive Innovation Is Key To Growth

ARK is a global asset manager specializing in thematic investing in disruptive innovation. The firm is rooted in over 40 years of experience in investing in technologies that aim to deliver outsized growth as industries transform.

Through its open research process, ARK seeks to identify companies that are leading and benefiting from cross-sector innovations such as artificial intelligence, robotics, energy storage, DNA sequencing, and blockchain technology.
